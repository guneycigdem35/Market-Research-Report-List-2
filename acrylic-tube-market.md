<p><strong>The Acrylic Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded</li><li>Cast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic tubes come in two main market types: extruded and cast. Extruded acrylic tubes are made by pushing melted acrylic through a die, resulting in a seamless and glossy finish. These tubes are more cost-effective and have higher dimensional stability. Cast acrylic tubes are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in a higher level of clarity and better chemical resistance. They are also more flexible in terms of shape and size customization. Both types are widely used in various industries for applications such as lighting, displays, and aquariums.</p></p>
