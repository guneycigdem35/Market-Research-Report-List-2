<p><ul><li>Sex Pheromones</li><li>Aggregation Pheromones</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural insect pheromones are categorized into three main types: sex pheromones, aggregation pheromones, and others. Sex pheromones are released by female insects to attract males for mating. Aggregation pheromones are emitted by both male and female insects to gather individuals in a specific area. Other types of pheromones include alarm pheromones, trail pheromones, and territorial pheromones, which serve different purposes in insect communication and behavior. These pheromones play a crucial role in pest management strategies in agriculture.</p></p>
