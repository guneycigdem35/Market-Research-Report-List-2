<p><strong>The Agriculture Adjuvants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Activator Adjuvants</li><li>Utility Adjuvants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agriculture adjuvants can be categorized into two main types: activator adjuvants and utility adjuvants. Activator adjuvants help enhance the effectiveness of pesticides by improving their absorption or penetration into plants. They also help in spreading and sticking the pesticide on the plant surface. Utility adjuvants, on the other hand, are used to modify the physical properties of the spray solution, such as reducing drift or foaming. Both types of adjuvants play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and performance of agricultural inputs.</p></p>
