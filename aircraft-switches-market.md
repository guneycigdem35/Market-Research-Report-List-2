<p><p>Aircraft switches market can be categorized into two main types: manual switches and automatic switches. Manual switches require physical operation by a pilot or crew member to turn on/off or adjust the settings of different systems in the aircraft. On the other hand, automatic switches are designed to function without manual intervention, using sensors or programmed logic to activate or adjust the systems as needed. Both types of switches play a crucial role in controlling various functions within an aircraft to ensure safe and efficient operations.</p></p>
