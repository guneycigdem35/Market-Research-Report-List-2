<p><p>Analog time switches are devices used to control electrical devices based on time settings. There are two main types of analog time switches on the market: plug-in analog time switches and in-wall analog time switches. Plug-in analog time switches are easily portable and can be used by simply plugging them into an electrical outlet. In-wall analog time switches are hardwired into a wall for a more permanent installation. Both types of analog time switches are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
