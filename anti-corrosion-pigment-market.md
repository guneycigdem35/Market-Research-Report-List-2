<p><p>Anti-corrosion pigment market is divided into two types: Phosphates based and Silica based. Phosphates based pigments are highly effective in preventing rust formation on metal surfaces by forming a protective layer. They are commonly used in industrial coatings for machinery and equipment. On the other hand, Silica based pigments provide excellent corrosion resistance by acting as a barrier between the metal surface and the external environment. They are commonly used in marine coatings to protect ships and offshore structures from rust and corrosion.</p></p>
