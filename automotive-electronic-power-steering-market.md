<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Electronic Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive electronic power steering systems come in three main types: column-mounted (C-EPS), pinion-mounted (P-EPS), and rack-mounted (R-EPS). C-EPS systems have the motor mounted on the steering column, providing a direct steering response. P-EPS systems have the motor mounted on the steering gear, offering a more compact design. R-EPS systems have the motor mounted directly on the rack, providing precise steering control. Each type of EPS system offers unique advantages in terms of response, design, and control.</p></p>
