<p><p>Automotive Expansion Valves are categorized into three main types: C Type Thermostatic Expansion Valve (TXM Valve), G Type Thermostatic Expansion Valve (TXV Valve), and V Type Thermostatic Expansion Valve (TMX Valve). The C type valve is suitable for small capacity systems, the G type is used in medium capacity systems, and the V type is ideal for large capacity systems. Each type of expansion valve regulates the flow of refrigerant based on temperature to ensure efficient cooling in automotive air conditioning systems.</p></p>
