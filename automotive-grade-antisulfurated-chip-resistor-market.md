<p><p>Automotive Grade AntiSulfurated Chip Resistor Market consists of two types: Thick Film Chip Resistor and Thin Film Chip Resistor. Thick Film Chip Resistors are made by depositing a resistance layer on a ceramic substrate, offering high stability and reliability at a lower cost. Thin Film Chip Resistors, on the other hand, are produced by depositing a thin resistive film on a ceramic substrate, providing higher precision and stability but at a higher cost. Both types are widely used in automotive electronics to prevent damage from sulfur-containing environments.</p></p>
