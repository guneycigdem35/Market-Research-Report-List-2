<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) System</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive keyless entry access systems are commonly divided into two types: Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) System and Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) System. PKE systems allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without taking the key out of their pocket or bag, using sensors to detect the key's presence. On the other hand, RKE systems require the driver to press a button on a remote control to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle from a short distance away.</p></p>
