<p><p>The automotive lubricants aftermarket market can be categorized into three main types: engine oil, gear oil, and transmission fluid. Engine oil is used to lubricate and protect the engine components, ensuring smooth operation and reducing wear. Gear oil is designed specifically for gearbox systems to provide proper lubrication and cooling. Transmission fluid is used in automatic transmission systems to optimize gear shifting and protect against overheating. Each type of lubricant plays a vital role in maintaining the performance and longevity of a vehicle's key components.</p></p>
