<p><ul><li>Manual Seat Recliners</li><li>Power Seat Recliners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive seat recliners can be divided into two main types: manual seat recliners and power seat recliners. Manual seat recliners are adjusted by using a lever or knob located on the side of the seat, allowing the driver or passenger to manually change the seat angle for their comfort. In contrast, power seat recliners are controlled electronically, usually through buttons on the side of the seat or the vehicle's dashboard, allowing for a more convenient and precise adjustment of the seat position.</p></p>
