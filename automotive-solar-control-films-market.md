<p><strong>The Automotive Solar Control Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clear (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Dyed (Non-Reflective)</li><li>Vacuum Coated (Reflective)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive solar control films come in three main types - Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films provide UV protection without altering the appearance of the glass. Dyed films offer increased heat reduction while maintaining a non-reflective look. Vacuum Coated films have a reflective surface that reduces heat and glare significantly. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing options for consumers to choose the best solution for their vehicle.</p></p>
