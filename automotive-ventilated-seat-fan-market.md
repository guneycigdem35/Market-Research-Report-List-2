<p><strong>The Automotive Ventilated Seat Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Radial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axial fans in the automotive ventilated seat fan market are characterized by their blades that rotate around an axis, providing a high airflow with a relatively low pressure. These fans are commonly used for cooling purposes in automotive seats. Radial fans, on the other hand, have blades that rotate outwards from the center of the fan, generating a higher pressure compared to axial fans. They are often used in automotive ventilated seats for more efficient air circulation and cooling. Both types of fans play a crucial role in maintaining comfort and temperature control in vehicles.</p></p>
