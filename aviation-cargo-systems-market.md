CLS are used to efficiently load and unload cargo from aircraft, ensuring safe and timely delivery. Management Systems track and oversee the movement of cargo, including inventory management and logistics. Transport Systems facilitate the physical transportation of cargo, whether it be by road, air, or sea. These systems work together to streamline the handling and delivery of cargo in the aviation industry.</p></p>
