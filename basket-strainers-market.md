<p><p>Basket strainers are essential components in industrial pipelines for filtering out solid particles to prevent damage to equipment. There are two main types in the market - Simplex and Duplex basket strainers. Simplex basket strainers have a single straining element for basic filtration needs, while Duplex basket strainers have two separate straining elements that allow for continuous operation by switching between them for cleaning or maintenance. Both types are crucial for ensuring the smooth operation and longevity of industrial equipment.</p></p>
