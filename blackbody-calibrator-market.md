<p><p>Blackbody calibrators are used to provide a reference temperature for infrared thermometers and thermal imagers. Hot plate-type blackbody calibrators use a heating element to generate a stable and uniform temperature across a surface. Cavity-type blackbody calibrators are enclosed chambers that emit infrared radiation to provide a reference temperature. The hot plate-type calibrators are more commonly used for lower temperature ranges, while cavity-type calibrators are typically used for higher temperature ranges due to their ability to reach higher temperatures and maintain stability.</p></p>
