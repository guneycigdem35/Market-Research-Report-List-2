<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Blister Packing Coating</li><li>Water-based Blister Packing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blister Packing Coating is available in two main types: Solvent-based and Water-based. Solvent-based coatings are formulated using organic solvents which evaporate during the drying process, leaving a smooth and uniform coating on the blister pack surface. On the other hand, Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly alternatives, containing water as the main solvent. They offer similar performance to solvent-based coatings but with lower levels of volatile organic compounds, making them a preferred choice for many industries.</p></p>
