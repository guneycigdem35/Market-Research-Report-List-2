<p><p>The bonded ceramic magnet market is segmented into three main types based on manufacturing processes: extruding, injection molding, and calendering. Extruding involves pushing material through a die to create a specific shape. Injection molding uses a mold to form molten material into the desired shape. Calendering presses the material between rollers to create thin sheets. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries, providing options for manufacturers to choose the most suitable production method for their specific requirements.</p></p>
