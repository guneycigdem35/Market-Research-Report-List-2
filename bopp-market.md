<p><p>BOPP market types include Metal Oxides Coated Type and Metal Coated Type. The Metal Oxides Coated Type BOPP film is enhanced with metal oxide coatings for improved barrier properties and heat resistance. On the other hand, Metal Coated Type BOPP film is coated with a layer of metal for enhanced barrier properties and added strength. Both types are commonly used in packaging applications where high barrier properties are necessary to protect the contents from moisture, oxygen, and other external factors.</p></p>
