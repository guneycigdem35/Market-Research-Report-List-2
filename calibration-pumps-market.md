<p><p>Calibration pumps are essential tools used in various industries to calibrate pressure instruments. There are two main types of calibration pumps: hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic calibration pumps use hydraulic pressure to generate precise pressure outputs for calibration purposes. On the other hand, pneumatic calibration pumps use compressed air to achieve the desired pressure levels. Both types of pumps are crucial for ensuring the accuracy and reliability of pressure instruments in industries such as oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
