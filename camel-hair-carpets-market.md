<p><ul><li>Dromedary Camel Hair</li><li>Bactrian Camel Hair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camel hair carpets come in two main market types: Dromedary camel hair and Bactrian camel hair. Dromedary camels have a single hump and are mostly found in North Africa and the Middle East, producing a softer and finer quality of hair. Bactrian camels, with two humps, are native to Central Asia and produce a coarser and thicker hair. Dromedary camel hair carpets are often more luxurious and expensive, while Bactrian camel hair carpets are more durable and suitable for high-traffic areas.</p></p>
