<p><p>Car ferries are vessels specifically designed to carry automobiles across bodies of water. There are two main types of car ferries in the market: Monohull and Multihull. Monohull ferries have a single hull structure, offering stability and efficiency in calm waters. On the other hand, Multihull ferries have multiple hulls, providing better stability and maneuverability in rough seas. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the ferry route and conditions of the water body being traversed.</p></p>
