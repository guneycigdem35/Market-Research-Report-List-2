<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN</li><li>Pitch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon fiber technology is categorized into two main market types: PAN-based carbon fiber and pitch-based carbon fiber. PAN-based carbon fiber is produced from polyacrylonitrile precursor, which is the most commonly used method due to its high mechanical properties and low production cost. On the other hand, pitch-based carbon fiber is derived from coal tar or petroleum pitch, resulting in fibers with higher thermal conductivity and resistance to chemical corrosion. Both market types offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industries like aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
