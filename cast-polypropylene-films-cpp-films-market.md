<p><p>Cast Polypropylene Films (CPP Films) are a type of plastic packaging material that is widely used due to its excellent moisture barrier properties. CPP Films come in two main types: Clear Films and Opaque Films. Clear Films are transparent and commonly used for packaging applications where the product needs to be visible. Opaque Films, on the other hand, are non-transparent and are often used for packaging applications where blocking light or concealing the product is necessary. Both types of CPP Films are highly versatile and offer great protection to a wide range of products.</p></p>
