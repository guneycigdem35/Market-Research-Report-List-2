<p><ul><li>Electronically Steered</li><li>Mechanically Steered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial flat panel antennas come in two main types: Electronically Steered and Mechanically Steered. Electronically Steered antennas use electronic components to adjust the direction of the antenna beam, offering more precise control and faster response times. On the other hand, Mechanically Steered antennas use physical mechanisms to adjust the direction of the antenna beam, offering simplicity and reliability. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
