<p><p>Composites consumables such as Film, Peel Plies, Flow Nettings, and Surfacing Veils are essential materials used in the production of composite materials. Films are used for vacuum bagging, Peel Plies help create a smooth surface finish, Flow Nettings assist in the distribution of resin, and Surfacing Veils provide a smooth finish and improve strength. These consumables play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and performance of composite products by assisting in the manufacturing process and enhancing the final appearance and properties of the composites.</p></p>
