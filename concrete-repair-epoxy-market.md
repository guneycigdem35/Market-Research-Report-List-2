<p><p>Concrete repair epoxy consists of two main types: hardener and activator. The hardener is a crucial component that initiates the curing process of the epoxy, leading to the formation of a strong and durable bond. On the other hand, the activator is responsible for controlling the curing time and the strength development of the epoxy. Both the hardener and activator play a significant role in ensuring the effectiveness and longevity of concrete repair projects.</p></p>
