<p><p>Conducting polymers are divided into two main types: electrically conducting and thermally conducting. Electrically conducting polymers can conduct electricity, making them useful in applications such as flexible electronics and sensors. Both types of conducting polymers have unique properties that make them valuable in various industries, from electronics to aerospace.</p></p>
