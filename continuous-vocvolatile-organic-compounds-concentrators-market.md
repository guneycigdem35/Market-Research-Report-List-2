<p><p>Continuous VOC Concentrators are used to remove volatile organic compounds from industrial processes. Zeolite VOC Concentrators utilize a porous mineral to adsorb VOCs, while Activated Carbon VOC Concentrators use activated carbon to adsorb VOCs. Zeolite concentrators are effective for low to medium VOC concentrations, while activated carbon concentrators are better for high VOC concentrations. Both types of concentrators are widely used in various industries to maintain air quality and meet regulatory standards for emissions control.</p></p>
