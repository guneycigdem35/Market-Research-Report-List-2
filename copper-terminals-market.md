<p><p>Copper terminals are commonly available in two main market types: Blocking Oil Type and Tube Pressure Type. Tube Pressure Type terminals are designed to be crimped onto wires under high pressure, ensuring a secure and reliable connection. Both types offer different features and advantages, catering to a wide range of applications in the electrical and electronics industry.</p></p>
