<p><strong>The Corn Fiber Gum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CGF I</li><li>CGF II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corn fiber gum, also known as corn gluten fiber I (CGF I) and corn gluten fiber II (CGF II), are two types of soluble fibers derived from corn. CGF I is usually lower in protein content and higher in fiber content compared to CGF II. Both types of corn fiber gum have various applications in the food industry as natural thickeners and stabilizers due to their ability to improve texture and moisture retention in products. CGF I is often used in bakery products, while CGF II is commonly used in beverages and dairy products.</p></p>
