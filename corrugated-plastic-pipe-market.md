<p><p>Corrugated plastic pipe is available in two main types - Single Wall Corrugated Pipe and Double Wall Corrugated Pipe. Single Wall Corrugated Pipe is lighter, more flexible, and generally less expensive, making it ideal for non-pressurized drainage applications. Double Wall Corrugated Pipe, on the other hand, offers more strength and durability due to its dual-layer construction, making it suitable for more demanding underground applications, such as storm sewers and culverts. Both types of pipe are commonly used in construction, agriculture, and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
