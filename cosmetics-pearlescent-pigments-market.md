<p><p>Cosmetics pearlescent pigments are available in two main types: powder and liquid. Powder pigments are finely milled particles that can be easily mixed into various cosmetic products such as eyeshadows, highlighters, and blushes. On the other hand, liquid pigments are pre-dispersed in a liquid medium and are often used in formulations like lip glosses and liquid eyeliners. Both types of pigments provide a shimmery, iridescent effect to cosmetics, enhancing their overall appearance and appeal to consumers.</p></p>
