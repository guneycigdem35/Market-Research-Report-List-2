<p><ul><li>Unmanned Ground Robots (UGR)</li><li>Unmanned Aerial Robots (UAR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crop maintenance robots can be categorized into two main types: Unmanned Ground Robots (UGR) and Unmanned Aerial Robots (UAR). UGRs are designed to operate on the ground, carrying out tasks such as weeding, planting, and spraying pesticides. UARs, on the other hand, are aerial drones that can monitor crop health from above, detect pests and diseases, and even spray pesticides or fertilizers. Both types of robots are revolutionizing the agricultural industry by increasing efficiency, reducing labor costs, and minimizing the use of chemicals.</p></p>
