<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep penetrating primers are divided into two main market types: one-component and two-component primers. One-component primers are ready to use straight out of the container and do not require any mixing with a catalyst or activator before application. They are typically easier to use and are more convenient for smaller projects. Two-component primers consist of separate components that must be mixed together before use. This ensures a more durable and long-lasting finish, making them ideal for larger and more demanding applications.</p></p>
