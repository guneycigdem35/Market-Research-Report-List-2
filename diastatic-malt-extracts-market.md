<p><p>Diastatic malt extracts are available in two main market types: liquid malt extract and dry malt extract. Liquid malt extract is a syrup-like substance that is made by evaporating malt extract to a liquid state. It is commonly used in home brewing and baking. Dry malt extract, on the other hand, is a powdered form of malt extract that has had the moisture removed. It is easier to store and has a longer shelf life than liquid malt extract. Both types are widely used for their fermentable sugars and flavor-enhancing properties in various applications.</p></p>
