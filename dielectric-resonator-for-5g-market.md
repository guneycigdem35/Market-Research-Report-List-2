<p><ul><li>TEM Mode</li><li>TE Mode</li><li>TM Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dielectric resonators for 5G are used for high-frequency applications in wireless communication. There are three types of modes in which these resonators operate: TEM mode, TE mode, and TM mode. In TEM mode, the electric and magnetic fields are perpendicular to the direction of wave propagation. In TE mode, the electric field is transverse to the direction of wave propagation. In TM mode, the magnetic field is transverse to the direction of wave propagation. Each mode offers different advantages for specific applications in 5G technology.</p></p>
