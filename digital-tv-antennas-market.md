<p><p>Digital TV antennas are available in two main types: Outdoor Digital TV antennas and Indoor Digital TV antennas. Outdoor antennas are designed to be mounted outside the home or building, offering a stronger signal reception due to their larger size and elevated position. On the other hand, Indoor antennas are smaller and sleeker, designed to be placed inside the home near the TV. They are suitable for areas with strong signal coverage and provide a convenient and discreet option for accessing digital television channels.</p></p>
