<p><p>The disposable toothbrush market is divided into two main types: electric toothbrushes and manual toothbrushes. Electric toothbrushes are powered devices that move the bristles in a circular motion to clean teeth more effectively. They are often preferred by those looking for a more thorough clean or individuals with mobility issues. Manual toothbrushes, on the other hand, rely on the user's own brushing movements to clean teeth. They are a more traditional option and tend to be more cost-effective than electric toothbrushes.</p></p>
