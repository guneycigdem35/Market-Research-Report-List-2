<p><strong>The Double Throw Safety Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-fusible Disconnect</li><li>Fusible Disconnect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-fusible disconnect double throw safety switches are used in applications where the load needs to be completely shut off without the need for overcurrent protection. They are commonly used in industrial settings where safety is a top priority. Fusible disconnect double throw safety switches, on the other hand, include fuses for overcurrent protection in addition to disconnecting the load. They are commonly used in situations where there is a risk of overcurrent damaging the equipment. Both types provide a safe way to disconnect power for maintenance or emergency situations.</p></p>
