<p><strong>The Drainage Geocomposite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geotextile-geonet</li><li>Geotextile-geocore</li><li>Geotextile-geogrid</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drainage geocomposite market types include geotextile-geonet, geotextile-geocore, geotextile-geogrid, and others. Geotextile-geonet composites consist of a geotextile layer bonded to a geonet, providing filtration and drainage capabilities. Geotextile-geocore composites have a geotextile outer layer encasing a drainage core, offering both filtration and protection. Geotextile-geogrid composites combine geotextiles with geogrids for enhanced drainage and reinforcement. Other types may include combinations of geotextiles with different materials to suit specific drainage needs.</p></p>
