<p><p>The electrical plastic market can be categorized into two main types: halogenated and non-halogenated. Halogenated electrical plastics contain halogen elements like chlorine and fluorine which provide fire-resistant properties. Non-halogenated plastics, on the other hand, do not contain these elements and are considered more environmentally friendly. While halogenated plastics offer superior fire resistance, non-halogenated plastics are preferred in applications where environmental considerations are a priority. Both types of electrical plastics have their own unique characteristics and applications in the market.</p></p>
