<p><p>Surface applied door closers are a type of electromagnetic door closer that is installed on the surface of the door frame. They are convenient to install and maintain, making them a popular choice for many buildings. Floor springs, on the other hand, are installed within the floor beneath the door, providing a sleek and minimalistic appearance. They are typically used in high-end or heavy-duty applications where aesthetics are important. Both types of door closers use electromagnetism to control the closing speed of the door.</p></p>
