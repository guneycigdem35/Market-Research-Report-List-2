<p><p>Electronic Health Record (EHR) software solutions are available in two main market types: Cloud-based and On-premise. Cloud-based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing for easier accessibility and scalability. On the other hand, On-premise EHR systems are installed and managed on the healthcare provider's own local servers. While Cloud-based solutions offer greater flexibility and cost-effectiveness, On-premise solutions provide greater control and security for sensitive patient data.</p></p>
