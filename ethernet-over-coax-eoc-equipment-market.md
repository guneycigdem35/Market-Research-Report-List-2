<p><p>Ethernet over Coax (EoC) equipment market includes two main types: Passive EoC and Active EoC. Passive EoC systems use existing coaxial cabling without the need for additional power sources, making them cost-effective and easy to install. Active EoC systems, on the other hand, require powered transceivers to boost the signal, resulting in higher performance and longer transmission distances. Both types cater to different needs and budgets, offering solutions for various applications in the networking industry.</p></p>
