<p><ul><li>PTC Sensor</li><li>NTC Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) sensors are classified into two types: Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) sensors and Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) sensors. PTC sensors have a resistance that increases with temperature, while NTC sensors have a resistance that decreases with temperature. PTC sensors are typically used for applications where higher temperatures are involved, such as in diesel engines, while NTC sensors are used for applications where lower temperatures are present, such as in automotive exhaust systems. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling exhaust gas temperatures in various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
