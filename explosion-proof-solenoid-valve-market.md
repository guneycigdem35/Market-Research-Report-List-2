<p><p>Explosion-proof solenoid valves are categorized based on the number of ports they have for controlling the flow of fluids. Two-way solenoid valves have an inlet and outlet port for controlling the flow of a single fluid. Three-way solenoid valves have three ports for directing the flow of two different fluids. Four-way solenoid valves have four ports for controlling the flow of two fluids in different directions. Each type of solenoid valve is designed for specific applications where precise control of fluid flow is required in hazardous environments.</p></p>
