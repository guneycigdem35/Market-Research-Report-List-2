<p><p>Fiber Grade Polylactic Acid (PLA) is a type of biodegradable polymer that is commonly used in the production of filaments for various applications. PLA can be categorized into two main types based on the structure of the filaments produced: Monofilament and Multifilament. Monofilament PLA is a single, continuous filament that is commonly used in 3D printing and textile industries. On the other hand, Multifilament PLA consists of multiple smaller filaments twisted together, offering higher strength and durability, making it suitable for applications requiring more robust materials.</p></p>
