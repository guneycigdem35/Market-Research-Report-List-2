<p><p>Fire Fighting Foam Concentrate Market consists of two main types- Class A Foam and Class B Foam. Class A Foam is mainly used for combating fires involving solid materials like wood, paper, and cloth, while Class B Foam is utilized for flammable liquids such as gasoline, diesel, and oil. Class A Foam works by reducing surface tension and cooling the fuel, while Class B Foam creates a blanket to prevent oxygen from reaching the fire. Both types of foam concentrates play a crucial role in firefighting operations.</p></p>
