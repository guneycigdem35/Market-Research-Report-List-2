Fittings, on the other hand, are the components used to connect and secure the pipes together, such as elbows, tees, and couplings.</p><p>Both pipes and fittings play a crucial role in ensuring the effectiveness and reliability of fire sprinkler systems in protecting buildings and occupants from fire hazards.</p></p>
