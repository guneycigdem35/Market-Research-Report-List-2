<p><p>Flexible pendulum accelerometers are devices used to measure acceleration by utilizing the movement of a flexible pendulum. There are two main types of flexible pendulum accelerometers on the market - piezoelectric type and piezoresistive type. Piezoelectric accelerometers generate an electrical charge in response to acceleration, while piezoresistive accelerometers change their resistance based on acceleration. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for accurate measurement of acceleration.</p></p>
