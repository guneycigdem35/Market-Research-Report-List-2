<p><p>Food contact paper and board market can be classified into two major types: poly coated and non-poly coated. Poly coated paper and board have a layer of polyethylene coating which provides resistance to water, grease, and moisture, making them suitable for packaging food products such as fast food items. On the other hand, non-poly coated paper and board do not have a polyethylene coating and are used for packaging dry food items such as bakery products and confectionery. Both types are widely used in the food packaging industry.</p></p>
