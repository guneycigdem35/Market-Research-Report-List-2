<p><p>Food ultrasonic testing systems can be classified into two main types based on the intensity of the ultrasound waves used: low intensity and high intensity. Low intensity systems are typically used for non-destructive testing of food products to detect defects or inconsistencies without altering the food's properties. On the other hand, high intensity systems are used for more complex applications such as cutting, emulsifying, and extracting substances from food products. Each type of system offers unique benefits and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
