<p><strong>The Gas Scrubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas scrubbers are categorized into two main types - Type I and Type II. Type I gas scrubbers focus on removing particulate matter from gases, such as dust and mist, using techniques like impingement and filtration. On the other hand, Type II gas scrubbers are designed to remove gases and odors from industrial processes through absorption or chemical reaction. Type I scrubbers are commonly used in industries like mining and metal processing, while Type II scrubbers are prevalent in chemical processing and wastewater treatment facilities.</p></p>
