<p><p>Glue-Laminated Timber (Glulam) is divided into two main market types: Monolayer Glulam and Multi-storey Glulam. Monolayer Glulam is a single layer of timber beams bonded together, commonly used in small-scale construction projects. Multi-storey Glulam, on the other hand, consists of multiple layers of timber beams bonded together, designed for larger-scale projects such as commercial buildings and bridges. Each market type serves a specific purpose in the construction industry, offering durability, strength, and sustainability.</p></p>
