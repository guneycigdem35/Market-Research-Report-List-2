<p><p>Glued parquet flooring can be classified into two main types: engineered and solid. Engineered parquet flooring is made up of multiple layers of wood pressed together, providing added stability and resistance to humidity. This type of flooring is ideal for areas with fluctuating temperatures. On the other hand, solid parquet flooring is made from a single piece of wood and is known for its durability and long lifespan. Both types of glued parquet flooring offer a wide range of styles and finishes to suit various interior design preferences.</p></p>
