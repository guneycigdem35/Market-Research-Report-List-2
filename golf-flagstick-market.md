<p><strong>The Golf Flagstick Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Adjustable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The golf flagstick market can be divided into two main types: fixed and adjustable. Fixed flagsticks are permanently set at a specific height, generally around 7 feet, which is the standard height for most golf courses. Adjustable flagsticks, on the other hand, allow for customization of the height, typically ranging from 6 to 8 feet. Golf courses may choose between fixed or adjustable flagsticks based on factors such as aesthetics, regulations, and player preferences. Both types serve the same purpose of marking the location of the hole on the green.</p></p>
