<p><p>Graphene membranes can be classified into two main types: Single layer and Multi-layer membranes. Single layer membranes consist of a single layer of graphene and are known for their exceptional mechanical, electrical, and thermal properties. On the other hand, multi-layer membranes are composed of multiple layers of graphene stacked on top of each other. While single layer membranes offer high flexibility and transparency, multi-layer membranes provide enhanced strength and durability. Both types of membranes have a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
