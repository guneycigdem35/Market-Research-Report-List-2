<p><p>Graphitic steel rolls are made from high carbon alloy steel with graphite flakes embedded in the matrix, providing improved wear resistance and thermal conductivity. Semi-steel rolls are composed of a cast iron core with an outer layer of alloy steel, offering a balance between good wear resistance and toughness. Other types of rolls in the market may include adamite, high-speed steel, or other specialty materials tailored for specific rolling applications. Each type offers unique properties to meet the diverse needs of the rolling industry.</p></p>
