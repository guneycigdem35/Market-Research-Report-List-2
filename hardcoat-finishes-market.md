<p><p>Hardcoat finishes are classified into three types based on their properties and applications. Type I is a thin, dense coating ideal for decorative purposes and offers moderate abrasion resistance. Type II is a thicker coating, providing increased durability and scratch resistance, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Type III is the thickest and toughest coating, offering the highest level of protection against wear and tear, making it popular in industrial or military applications. Each type caters to specific market needs and requirements for long-lasting protection and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
