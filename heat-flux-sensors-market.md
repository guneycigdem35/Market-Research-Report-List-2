<p><strong>The Heat Flux Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convective Measurement</li><li>Radiative Measurement</li><li>Conductive Heat Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat flux sensors measure the rate of heat transfer through a surface. Convective measurement sensors detect heat transfer due to fluid flow, such as air or water. Radiative measurement sensors measure heat transfer through electromagnetic radiation. Conductive heat measurement sensors detect heat transfer through direct contact between surfaces. Each type of sensor has its own advantages and applications based on the specific heat transfer mechanisms being measured, making them essential tools in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and building services.</p></p>
