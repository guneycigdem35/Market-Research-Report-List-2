Metallic oxide compounds typically offer high thermal conductivity, while ceramic-based compounds provide good electrical insulation. Silicone compounds are known for their flexibility and ease of application. Other specialty compounds may offer unique properties such as higher temperature resistance or non-corrosive properties.
