<p><p>High-strength Phenolic Molding Compound has two main types in the market: Press Molding Compound and Injection Molding Compound. Press Molding Compound is a process where the compound is pressed into shape using a mold and high pressure, resulting in a strong and durable product. On the other hand, Injection Molding Compound involves injecting the compound into a mold under high pressure and heat, allowing for complex shapes and high precision. Both types are widely used in industries requiring high-strength and heat-resistant materials.</p></p>
