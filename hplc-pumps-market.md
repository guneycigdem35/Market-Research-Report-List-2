<p><p>HPLC pumps are broadly classified into two categories: Standard HPLC pumps and UHPLC pumps. Standard HPLC pumps are primarily used for routine applications where high resolution is not required, whereas UHPLC pumps are designed for ultra-high performance liquid chromatography applications that demand higher pressure and faster flow rates. UHPLC pumps are more advanced and offer improved efficiency and sensitivity compared to standard HPLC pumps. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in liquid chromatography separations in various industries.</p></p>
