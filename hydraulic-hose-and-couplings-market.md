<p><p>The hydraulic hose and couplings market can be classified into two main types: spiral wire and wire braided. Spiral wire hoses are constructed with multiple layers of spiral wire reinforcement for high-pressure applications. On the other hand, wire braided hoses consist of braided steel wires for lower to medium pressure applications. These hoses are essential components in hydraulic systems, transferring fluid power to various machinery and equipment. The choice between spiral wire and wire braided hoses depends on the specific pressure requirements of the application.</p></p>
