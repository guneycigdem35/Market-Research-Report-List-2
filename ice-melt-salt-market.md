<p><p>Ice melt salt market is categorized into two main types: liquid ice melt salt and solid ice melt salt. Liquid ice melt salt is typically in the form of a solution that can be sprayed or applied directly onto ice and snow-covered surfaces. Solid ice melt salt, on the other hand, comes in the form of granules or pellets that are spread or scattered over icy areas to accelerate melting. Both types are widely used for de-icing and snow melting purposes in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
