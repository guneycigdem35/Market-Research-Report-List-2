<p><p>Impervious Concrete is a type of concrete that has low permeability, preventing water from passing through. There are three main types of Impervious Concrete on the market: Ordinary Impervious Concrete, which is a basic form with low porosity; Admixture Impervious Concrete, which includes additives to further reduce permeability; and Expansive Impervious Concrete, which expands when in contact with water to create a tighter seal. Each type offers varying degrees of water resistance for different construction needs.</p></p>
