<p><p>The Industrial Control System (ICS) Security Market consists of three main types: SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition), DCS (Distributed Control System), and PLC (Programmable Logic Controller). SCADA systems are used to monitor and control processes in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and power generation. DCS systems are used for controlling complex processes in manufacturing industries. PLCs are programmable devices used to automate machinery in industries like automation, automotive, and manufacturing. Each of these market types plays a crucial role in ensuring the security of industrial control systems.</p></p>
