<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Printheads</li><li>Thermal Printheads</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial inkjet printheads market can be categorized into three main types- Piezoelectric printheads, thermal printheads, and others. Piezoelectric printheads use a piezoelectric crystal to control the droplet ejection process, while thermal printheads use a heating element to vaporize ink and create droplets. The "others" category includes various printhead technologies such as electrostatic, continuous inkjet, and MEMS printheads. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of printing speed, resolution, and cost-effectiveness, catering to diverse industrial printing needs.</p></p>
