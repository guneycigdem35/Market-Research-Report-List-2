<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet Spectrum Camera</li><li>Infrared Spectrum Camera</li><li>Visible Spectrum Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inspection cameras come in various types based on the spectrum they operate in. Ultraviolet spectrum cameras are used for inspecting objects that emit UV light, such as certain minerals or industrial materials. Infrared spectrum cameras detect heat signatures and are commonly used in electrical and mechanical inspections. Visible spectrum cameras, which operate in the normal range of human vision, are versatile and widely used for inspections in industries such as construction, automotive, and security. Each type of camera offers unique capabilities for different inspection needs.</p></p>
