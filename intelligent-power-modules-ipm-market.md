<p><p>Intelligent Power Modules (IPM) are integrated power devices that combine power semiconductor devices with control circuits in a single package. There are two main types of IPM available in the market: IGBT-IPM and MOSFET-IPM. IGBT-IPM utilizes insulated-gate bipolar transistors for high-voltage applications, while MOSFET-IPM uses metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors for lower voltage applications. Both types offer improved efficiency, reliability, and thermal performance compared to traditional discrete power devices, making them popular choices in the power electronics industry.</p></p>
