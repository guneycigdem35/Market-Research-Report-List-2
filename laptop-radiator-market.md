Active laptop radiators require power to operate and typically include fans to actively cool the laptop. On the other hand, passive laptop radiators do not require power and rely on natural airflow or heat dissipation to cool the laptop. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, depending on the user's needs and preferences.</p></p>
