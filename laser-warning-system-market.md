<p><p>Passive Laser Warning Systems detect laser radiation and alert the user without directly mitigating the threat. These systems are ideal for situations where avoidance is the primary goal. Active Laser Warning Systems also detect laser radiation but have the added capability of actively disrupting or neutralizing the threat.
