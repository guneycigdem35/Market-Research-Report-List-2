<p><strong>The LDI (Laser Direct Imaging) Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LDI machines are used in the manufacturing of printed circuit boards. There are two main types of LDI machines based on the laser used for imaging: Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm. The Polygon Mirror 365nm machines utilize a polygon mirror to direct a 365nm laser for precise imaging. On the other hand, DMD 405nm machines use a Digital Micromirror Device (DMD) to project a 405nm laser for high-resolution imaging. Both types of machines offer advanced capabilities for efficient and accurate PCB manufacturing.</p></p>
