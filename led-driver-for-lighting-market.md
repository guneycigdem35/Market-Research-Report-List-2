<p><strong>The LED Driver for Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current LED Drivers</li><li>Constant Voltage LED Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED drivers for lighting can be categorized into two main types: constant current LED drivers and constant voltage LED drivers. Constant current LED drivers are designed to deliver a stable current to the LED, ensuring consistent brightness and preventing overheating. On the other hand, constant voltage LED drivers supply a fixed voltage to the LED, allowing for flexibility in connecting multiple LEDs in parallel. Both types play a crucial role in powering LED lighting systems and are tailored to meet specific lighting requirements for various applications.</p></p>
