Resin type liquid shims offer strong bonding properties, rubber type liquid shims provide flexibility and shock absorption, oil modified type liquid shims offer resistance to oil and chemicals, and natural polymer type liquid shims are eco-friendly and biodegradable alternatives. Each type caters to specific application needs in various industries.</p></p>
