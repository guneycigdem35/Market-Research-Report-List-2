<p><p>Masterbatches are widely used in the plastics industry. The different types of masterbatches include White Masterbatch, Black Masterbatch, Color Masterbatch, Additive Masterbatch, and Plastic Filler Masterbatch. White Masterbatch provides opacity and brightness to the plastic, whereas Black Masterbatch adds darkness and UV protection. Color Masterbatch adds vibrant colors to the plastic, while Additive Masterbatch enhances specific properties like UV resistance or flame retardancy. Plastic Filler Masterbatch is used to reduce production costs by incorporating filler materials into the plastic.</p></p>
