<p><p>Medical Radiation Sterilization Equipment market is classified into three types based on the type of radiation used for sterilization. E-Beam Radiation Sterilization utilizes high-energy electrons to kill bacteria and viruses on medical equipment. X-Ray Sterilization uses X-rays to penetrate the material and destroy microorganisms. Gamma Sterilization utilizes gamma rays from a radioactive isotope to sterilize medical devices. Each type of radiation sterilization offers unique benefits and is commonly used in the healthcare industry to ensure the safety and efficacy of medical equipment.</p></p>
