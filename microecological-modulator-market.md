<p><p>Microecological modulators refer to substances that can modify the composition of microorganisms in the gut, ultimately benefiting human health. The three main types are probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics. Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria that can promote gut health, while prebiotics are non-digestible fibers that provide food for these beneficial bacteria. Synbiotics combine both probiotics and prebiotics to enhance the growth and survival of the beneficial bacteria in the gut. These products are increasingly popular in the health and wellness market due to their potential benefits for digestion and immune function.</p></p>
