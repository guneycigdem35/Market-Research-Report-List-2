<p><ul><li>Cold Work Die Steel</li><li>Hot Work Die Steel</li><li>Plastic Mould Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mold steel market can be categorized into three main types: Cold Work Die Steel, Hot Work Die Steel, and Plastic Mould Steel. Cold Work Die Steel is used for making tools and dies for shaping cold materials. Hot Work Die Steel is for applications that require high temperatures, like forging and casting. Plastic Mould Steel is specifically designed for making injection molds and other tools for plastic molding processes. Each type of steel is tailored to meet the specific demands of different manufacturing applications.</p></p>
