<p><p>Natural wax emulsion is a product that consists of wax particles dispersed in water. The market for natural wax emulsions can be classified into two main types: Paraffin Wax and Carnauba Wax. Paraffin wax emulsions are popular for their versatility and wide range of applications in industries such as cosmetics, packaging, and adhesives. On the other hand, Carnauba wax emulsions are known for their superior hardness and gloss, making them ideal for use in automotive waxes, polishes, and food packaging.</p></p>
