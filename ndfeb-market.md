<p><p>NdFeB magnets can be categorized into three main market types based on their manufacturing processes: sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets, bonded Nd-Fe-B magnets, and hot pressed NdFeB magnets. Sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets are produced through a powder metallurgy process, bonded Nd-Fe-B magnets are made using a resin or polymer to bind the magnetic particles together, and hot pressed NdFeB magnets are created by applying high-temperature and high-pressure techniques. Each type of NdFeB magnet has its own unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
