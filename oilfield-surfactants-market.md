<p><ul><li>Anionic Surfactant</li><li>Non-Ionic Surfactant</li><li>Cationic Surfactant</li><li>Amphoteric Surfactants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oilfield surfactants are chemicals used in oil extraction to reduce the surface tension between oil and water. They can be classified into four main types based on their ionic charge: anionic surfactants have a negative charge, non-ionic surfactants have no charge, cationic surfactants have a positive charge, and amphoteric surfactants have both positive and negative charges. Each type of surfactant is used in specific applications within the oilfield industry to help increase oil recovery efficiency and reduce production costs.</p></p>
