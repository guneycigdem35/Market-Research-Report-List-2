<p><ul><li>Polarization Dependent Opto-Isolators</li><li>Polarization Independent Opto-Isolators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Opto-isolators are used to protect electrical devices by isolating them from high voltage spikes and surges. There are two main types of opto-isolators in the market: polarization dependent and polarization independent. </p><p>Polarization dependent opto-isolators rely on the polarization of light for their operation, while polarization independent opto-isolators are not affected by the polarization of light. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safety and reliability of electronic systems in various industrial applications.</p></p>
