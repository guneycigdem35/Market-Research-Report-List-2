<p><p>Outdoor coolers come in two main types: hard coolers and soft coolers. Hard coolers are constructed with a rigid outer shell, making them durable and efficient at keeping items cold for extended periods of time. Soft coolers, on the other hand, are made with flexible materials like canvas or nylon, making them lightweight and easy to transport. Each type has its own advantages, with hard coolers being better for longer trips and soft coolers being more convenient for short outings.</p></p>
