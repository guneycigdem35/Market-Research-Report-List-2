<p><p>Oxidized PE wax is classified into three main types based on the manufacturing process and end use: Polymerization Oxidized PE Wax, Modification Oxidized PE Wax, and Thermal Cracking Oxidized PE Wax. Polymerization involves the reaction of PE wax with oxygen to form a polymer, while Modification involves the addition of various chemicals to alter the properties of PE wax. Thermal Cracking involves the breaking down of PE wax molecules at high temperatures to produce a different type of oxidized PE wax with unique characteristics.</p></p>
