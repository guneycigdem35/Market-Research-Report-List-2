<p><p>Paragliding harnesses are categorized into single and tandem harnesses based on the number of individuals they are designed to carry during a flight. Single harnesses are meant for solo paragliding flights, allowing only one person to be securely strapped in. On the other hand, tandem harnesses are specifically designed for tandem flights, where two people - typically an experienced pilot and a passenger - are safely attached together. Both single and tandem harnesses offer different features and designs to cater to the specific needs and preferences of paragliders.</p></p>
