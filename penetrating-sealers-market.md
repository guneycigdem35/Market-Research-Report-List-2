<p><p>Penetrating sealers are categorized into three main types: Silane, Siloxane, and Silane Siloxane Mixture. Silane sealers provide excellent protection against water intrusion due to their small molecular size, allowing them to penetrate deep into the substrate. Siloxane sealers create a water repellent barrier on the surface of the material, providing long-lasting protection. Silane Siloxane mixtures combine both types of sealers, offering a comprehensive solution for protecting various surfaces from moisture damage and deterioration.</p></p>
