<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask inspection equipment market consists of two main types of methods - Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). </p><p>In Die to Die (DD) method, the equipment compares the mask image to another mask image to detect defects. This method is used for identifying defects that occur during the manufacturing process.</p><p>On the other hand, Die to Database (DB) method compares the mask image to a reference database of the ideal image. This method is used for verifying the correctness of the design.</p></p>
