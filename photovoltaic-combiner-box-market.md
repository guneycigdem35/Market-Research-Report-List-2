<p><p>A photovoltaic combiner box is an electrical enclosure that combines the output from multiple solar panels into a single DC output. It typically contains fuses or circuit breakers to protect the photovoltaic system from overcurrent and overvoltage conditions. The main purpose of a combiner box is to simplify the wiring process and provide a centralized location for safety devices.</p><p>The photovoltaic combiner box market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for renewable energy sources, government incentives for solar power installations, and the rising awareness about environmental sustainability are driving the growth of the market.
