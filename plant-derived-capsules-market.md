<p><ul><li>HPMC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plant-derived capsules are categorized into two main types: HPMC capsules and other capsules. HPMC capsules are made from cellulose, a natural plant-derived material, making them suitable for vegetarians and vegans. They are also known for their low moisture content and stability. On the other hand, other plant-derived capsules are made from materials like pullulan, starch, or modified cellulose. These capsules also offer a natural and plant-based alternative to traditional gelatin capsules, appealing to consumers looking for eco-friendly and sustainable options.</p></p>
