<p><p>Plastics processing aids are chemicals added to plastic resins during the manufacturing process to enhance various properties. The types of plastics processing aids can be classified into different categories such as styrenes, acrylics, calcium carbonates, lubricants, silicone oils, and others. Styrenes are used to improve impact resistance, while acrylics improve clarity and color stability. Calcium carbonates act as fillers, lubricants aid in processing, and silicone oils enhance surface finish. Other processing aids may include antioxidants, UV stabilizers, and antistatic agents.</p></p>
