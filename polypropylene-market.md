<p><ul><li>Isotactic Polypropylene</li><li>Atactic Polypropylene</li><li>Syndiotactic Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene market is segmented into three types based on the structure of the polymer chains: Isotactic Polypropylene has a highly ordered arrangement of the polymer chains, Atactic Polypropylene has randomly arranged polymer chains, and Syndiotactic Polypropylene has alternating arrangement of polymer chains. Each type has different physical properties and applications in various industries such as automotive, packaging, and textiles. Isotactic PP is known for its high tensile strength, Atactic PP for its flexibility, and Syndiotactic PP for its stiffness and heat resistance.</p></p>
