<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane adhesive sealants come in two main types: one-component and two-component. One-component sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient for quick applications. Two-component sealants require mixing of two different components before application, offering better control over curing time and bond strength. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application, with one-component sealants being more suitable for simpler projects and two-component sealants being preferred for more complex and demanding applications.</p></p>
