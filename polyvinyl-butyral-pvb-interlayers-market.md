<p><p>Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) Interlayers are used in laminated glass to enhance safety and security. There are two main types of PVB interlayers: Standard Polyvinyl Butyral and Structural Polyvinyl Butyral. Standard PVB interlayers are commonly used in automotive and architectural applications to provide basic safety and sound insulation. On the other hand, Structural PVB interlayers are engineered to provide higher levels of strength and durability, making them suitable for high-security applications such as bulletproof glass and hurricane-resistant windows.</p></p>
