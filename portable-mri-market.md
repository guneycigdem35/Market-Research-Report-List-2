<p><strong>The Portable MRI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI</li><li>Electromagnet-based Portable MRI</li><li>Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable MRI machines come in different types based on the magnet technology used: superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI, electromagnet-based Portable MRI, and permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI. Superconducting-magnet-based Portable MRI machines provide high-quality images but require cryogen cooling systems, making them less portable. Electromagnet-based Portable MRI machines are more flexible and easier to transport, providing good image quality. Permanent-magnet-based Portable MRI machines are compact and easy to use but may have limitations in image quality compared to the other types.</p></p>
