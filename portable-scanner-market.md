<p><p>Portable scanners come in three main types: Laser Scanners, Linear Imagers, and 2D Imagers. Laser scanners use a laser beam to read the barcode by bouncing light off the code. Linear imagers capture the barcode image by taking a picture of the code and decoding it. 2D imagers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes and can capture information from mobile devices. Each type of portable scanner has its advantages and may be more suitable for specific applications depending on the type of scanning required.</p></p>
