<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controller</li><li>PoE Powered Devices (PD) Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Over Ethernet Controller Market consists of two main types: PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controller and PoE Powered Devices (PD) Controller. PSE controllers are responsible for delivering power to connected devices through Ethernet cables, while PD controllers receive power from PSE controllers to operate. PSE controllers are commonly found in Ethernet switches and midspans, while PD controllers are present in devices such as cameras, access points, and IP phones. Both controllers play a crucial role in enabling the efficient and effective delivery of power over Ethernet networks.</p></p>
