<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Rotor Locks</li><li>Electric Rotor Locks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic rotor locks utilize hydraulic pressure to securely immobilize rotors in place, ensuring safety during maintenance or storage. They are known for their high durability and reliability, making them a popular choice in various industries. On the other hand, electric rotor locks use electric power to lock and unlock rotors, offering convenience and ease of operation. Both types of rotor locks provide effective and efficient solutions for safely securing rotors in place, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
