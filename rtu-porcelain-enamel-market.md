<p><strong>The RTU Porcelain Enamel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enamel Powder</li><li>Enamel Frit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTU porcelain enamel market can be divided into two main types: enamel powder and enamel frit. Enamel powder is a finely ground mixture of glass and pigments that is applied to a surface and then baked to create a durable and decorative finish. Enamel frit, on the other hand, is a premixed powdered form of enamel that is melted onto a substrate at high temperatures to form a smooth and glossy coating. Both enamel powder and enamel frit offer a wide range of colors and finishes, making them popular choices for coating metal, glass, and ceramic surfaces in various industries.</p></p>
