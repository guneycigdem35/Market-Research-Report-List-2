<p><p>Rubberized Asphalt Concrete (RAC) is a type of asphalt pavement made by incorporating rubber from recycled tires into the asphalt mix. There are two main types of RAC markets: Asphalt-rubber and Terminal Blend. Asphalt-rubber involves blending the rubber with the hot asphalt before it is mixed with aggregate, while Terminal Blend involves blending the rubber with the asphalt at the terminal before delivery to the construction site. Both types of RAC offer benefits such as improved durability, reduced road noise, and enhanced skid resistance.</p></p>
