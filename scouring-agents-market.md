<p><strong>The Scouring Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic Scouring Agent</li><li>Cationic Scouring Agent</li><li>Nonionic Scouring Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scouring agents are used in textile processing to remove impurities, oils, and waxes from fabrics. There are three main types of scouring agents: anionic scouring agents, cationic scouring agents, and nonionic scouring agents. Anionic scouring agents are negatively charged and are effective at removing negatively charged contaminants. Cationic scouring agents are positively charged and work well on positively charged impurities. Nonionic scouring agents are uncharged and are versatile in their ability to remove a wide range of contaminants.</p></p>
