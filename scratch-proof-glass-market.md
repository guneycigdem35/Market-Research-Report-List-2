<p><p>Scratch proof glass comes in two main types - chemically-strengthened glass and sapphire glass. Chemically-strengthened glass is treated with chemicals to increase its durability and scratch resistance. It is commonly used in electronic devices and automotive applications. Sapphire glass, on the other hand, is made from synthetic sapphire and is even harder and more scratch-resistant than chemically-strengthened glass. It is often used in high-end watches, smartphones, and camera lenses for its superior scratch-proof properties.</p></p>
