<p><strong>The Shutter Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired Shutter Sensor</li><li>Wireless Shutter Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shutter sensors are available in two main types - wired and wireless. Wired shutter sensors require a physical connection to a central control panel, making them more suitable for new installations or renovations. On the other hand, wireless shutter sensors communicate with the control panel via radio frequency signals, offering greater flexibility in placement and easier installation without the need for wiring. Both types of sensors are commonly used in home security systems to detect unauthorized entry through windows or doors.</p></p>
