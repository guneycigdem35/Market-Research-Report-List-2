<p><strong>The Silver Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aluminized Glass Mirror</li><li>Silvered Glass Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminized glass mirrors are created by depositing a thin layer of aluminum onto one side of a glass surface, resulting in a highly reflective surface. Silvered glass mirrors, on the other hand, are made by coating the back of a glass surface with a layer of silver or other reflective material. Both types of mirrors are commonly used in commercial and residential applications, with aluminized glass mirrors typically being more durable and resistant to corrosion, while silvered glass mirrors often offer a higher level of reflectivity.</p></p>
