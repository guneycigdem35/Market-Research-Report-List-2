<p><ul><li>Single-Voltage Type</li><li>Dual-Voltage Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SLIC modules are available in two main market types: Single-Voltage Type and Dual-Voltage Type. Single-Voltage Type modules operate at a fixed voltage level, making them simpler and more cost-effective. They are suitable for applications where a single voltage source is sufficient. On the other hand, Dual-Voltage Type modules offer the flexibility of operating at two different voltage levels, allowing for increased compatibility with a wider range of devices and systems. This versatility comes at a slightly higher cost but provides added functionality for more complex applications.</p></p>
