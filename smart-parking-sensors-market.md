<p><p>Smart Parking Sensors provide efficient parking solutions for drivers by using advanced technology. Two main types of sensors include Electromagnetic Parking Sensors and Ultrasonic Reversing Sensors. Electromagnetic sensors detect the presence of a vehicle through changes in magnetic fields, while Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to measure distance between objects. Both types of sensors help drivers detect obstacles and maneuver into parking spaces safely, making parking easier and more convenient.</p></p>
