<p><p>Soil hardening agents are categorized into three types based on their physical form - flake, powder, and liquid. Flake soil hardening agents are solid pieces that can be easily spread over soil surfaces. Powder soil hardening agents are finely ground substances that can be mixed with soil to improve its stability. Liquid soil hardening agents are in liquid form and can be easily sprayed or poured onto soil surfaces to harden them. Each type offers different application methods and benefits for strengthening soil.</p></p>
