<p><p>Sonohysterography catheters are used for imaging the uterus and fallopian tubes. The single lumen catheter is simpler and easier to use, while the double lumen catheter allows for better visualization and drainage during the procedure. Both types of catheters play a vital role in diagnosing and monitoring gynecological conditions.</p></p>
