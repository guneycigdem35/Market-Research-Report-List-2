<p><p>Specialty admixtures are additives used in concrete to enhance its properties. The different types of specialty admixtures include corrosion inhibitors, which protect steel reinforcements from rusting; shrinkage control inhibitors, which reduce cracking in concrete due to drying shrinkage; integral color admixtures, which add color to concrete; and other admixtures like water reducers, accelerators, and retarding agents. Each type of admixture serves a specific purpose in improving the performance and durability of concrete structures.</p></p>
