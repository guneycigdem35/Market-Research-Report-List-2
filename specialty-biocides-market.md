<p><p>Specialty biocides market can be classified into two main types: inorganic biocides and organic biocides. Inorganic biocides are derived from non-carbon containing compounds such as silver, copper, and zinc. They are effective in controlling microbial growth in a wide range of applications. On the other hand, organic biocides are carbon-based compounds that are also effective in controlling microbial growth, but they are preferred in industries where environmental concerns are a priority. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring product preservation and protecting against harmful microorganisms.</p></p>
