<p><strong>The Spent Fuel Pool Level Instrumentation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Guided Wave Radar System</li><li>Through-Air Radar System</li><li>Bubbler System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spent Fuel Pool Level Instrumentation Systems are used in nuclear power plants to monitor the water levels in spent fuel pools. There are four main types of systems used for this purpose - Guided Wave Radar System, Through-Air Radar System, Bubbler System, and Others. Guided Wave Radar System uses radar waves guided along a probe to measure the level. Through-Air Radar System uses radar waves transmitted through the air to measure the level. Bubbler System uses air pressure to measure the level. Other systems may include ultrasonic or capacitance-based technologies.</p></p>
