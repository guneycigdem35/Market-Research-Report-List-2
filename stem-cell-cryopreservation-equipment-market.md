<p><ul><li>Liquid Phase</li><li>Vapor Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stem cell cryopreservation equipment market types include liquid phase and vapor phase systems. Liquid phase systems involve storing stem cells in a liquid nitrogen-filled chamber at ultra-low temperatures. These systems are known for their stability and reliable long-term storage. Vapor phase systems, on the other hand, use liquid nitrogen to create vapor that surrounds the stem cells, providing an effective method of cryopreservation. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in preserving stem cells for research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
