<p><p>Supermarket turnstiles can be categorized into different types based on their design and mechanism. Arm turnstiles feature rotating arms that allow one person to pass through at a time. Swing gates are hinged barriers that swing open in one direction to permit entry. Waist-high helicopter turnstiles have rotating horizontal bars at waist height. Other types include optical barriers and full-height turnstiles. Each type of turnstile has its own advantages and is suitable for different environments and levels of security requirements.</p></p>
