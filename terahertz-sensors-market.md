<p><p>Terahertz sensors are utilized in both photonic sensing and electronic sensing markets. Photonic sensing involves using light to detect and measure objects, while electronic sensing involves using electrical signals for detection. In the photonic sensing market, terahertz sensors provide high-resolution imaging and spectroscopy capabilities for various applications such as security screening and medical imaging. In the electronic sensing market, terahertz sensors are used for non-destructive testing, quality control, and material characterization due to their ability to penetrate opaque materials and provide detailed information.</p></p>
