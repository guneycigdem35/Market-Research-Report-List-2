<p><strong>The TFT Array Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TFT Positive Photoresist</li><li>TFT Negative Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TFT Array Photoresist market consists of two main types: TFT Positive Photoresist and TFT Negative Photoresist. Positive photoresist is used in the production of thin-film transistors (TFTs) by exposing areas that are meant to remain on the substrate. Negative photoresist, on the other hand, is used by exposing areas that are meant to be removed. Both types play a crucial role in the manufacturing of TFT arrays, enabling precise patterning and etching processes in the production of electronic displays and other devices.</p></p>
