<p><p>TFT LCD Display market types include Twisted Nematic (TN), In-Plane Switching (IPS), Advanced Fringe Field Switching (AFFS), Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment (MVA), Patterned Vertical Alignment (PVA), Advanced Super View (ASV), Plane Line Switching (PLS), and others. TN displays are known for their fast response times, while IPS offers superior color accuracy and viewing angles. AFFS provides better sunlight readability, MVA and PVA offer improved contrast ratios, ASV enhances viewing angles, and PLS combines the benefits of IPS and TN technologies. Others refer to niche display technologies catering to specific requirements.</p></p>
