<p><ul><li>Uncooled Thermal Imaging</li><li>Cryogenic Cooling Thermal Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Imaging Sight market is divided into two types: Uncooled Thermal Imaging and Cryogenic Cooling Thermal Imaging. Uncooled Thermal Imaging technology uses infrared sensors that do not require cooling, making them more cost-effective and energy-efficient. On the other hand, Cryogenic Cooling Thermal Imaging involves cooling the sensors to very low temperatures for enhanced image quality and sensitivity. While uncooled thermal imaging is popular for its affordability, cryogenic cooling thermal imaging offers superior performance in terms of image clarity and thermal sensitivity.</p></p>
