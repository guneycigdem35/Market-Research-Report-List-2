<p><strong>The Tool Spindles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear-driven Spindles</li><li>Belt-driven Spindles</li><li>Direct-driven Spindles</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tool spindles market can be classified into various types based on their drive mechanisms, including gear-driven spindles, belt-driven spindles, direct-driven spindles, and others. Gear-driven spindles use gears to transfer power from the motor to the spindle, providing high torque and accuracy. Belt-driven spindles utilize belts to transfer power, offering flexibility in speed control. Direct-driven spindles have the motor directly connected to the spindle, ensuring faster response time and higher efficiency. Other types may include hydrostatic or air-driven spindles.</p></p>
