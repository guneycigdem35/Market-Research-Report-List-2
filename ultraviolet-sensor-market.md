<p><p>Ultraviolet sensors can be categorized into three main types: UVA sensors, UVB sensors, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors mainly detect long-wave ultraviolet radiation, which can cause skin aging and wrinkles. UVB sensors detect medium-wave ultraviolet radiation, responsible for sunburn and skin cancer. UVC sensors detect short-wave ultraviolet radiation, which is mostly absorbed by the Earth's atmosphere. Each type of sensor serves a specific purpose in monitoring and controlling UV exposure levels in various applications such as sunscreen testing, disinfection, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
