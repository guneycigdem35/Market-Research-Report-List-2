<p><p>Vacuum salt is available in two main market types: granular vacuum salt and fine vacuum salt. Granular vacuum salt is made up of larger crystals that are ideal for industrial applications such as water softening, de-icing, and chemical manufacturing. Fine vacuum salt, on the other hand, consists of smaller crystals that are commonly used in food processing, pharmaceuticals, and other applications that require a quick dissolving salt. Both types of vacuum salt offer high purity and consistent quality, making them versatile options for various industries.</p></p>
