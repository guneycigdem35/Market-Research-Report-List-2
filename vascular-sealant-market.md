<p><p>Vascular sealants are used in surgical procedures to stop bleeding and promote tissue healing and sealing. There are two main types of vascular sealants: natural/biological sealants and synthetic sealants. Natural sealants are derived from human or animal proteins, such as collagen or fibrin, while synthetic sealants are made from synthetic polymers. Natural sealants are biocompatible and biodegradable, while synthetic sealants offer longer-lasting sealing properties. Both types play a crucial role in improving surgical outcomes and reducing complications.</p></p>
