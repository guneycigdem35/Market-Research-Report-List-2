<p><strong>The Vehicle Inertial Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acceleration Sensor</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>IMU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle inertial sensor market types include Acceleration Sensor, Gyroscope, and Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU). Acceleration sensors measure the rate of change of velocity of a vehicle, providing data on acceleration and deceleration. Gyroscopes measure the angular velocity of a vehicle, providing information on its orientation and rotation. IMUs combine both acceleration sensors and gyroscopes to provide comprehensive data on a vehicle's motion and position. These sensors play a crucial role in various automotive applications, such as stability control systems and autonomous driving technology.</p></p>
