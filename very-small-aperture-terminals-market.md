<p><ul><li>Single Channel Per Carrier (SCPC)</li><li>Multiple Channels Per Carrier (MCPC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Very Small Aperture Terminals (VSAT) technology can be categorized into two main market types: SCPC and MCPC. SCPC utilizes a dedicated single channel per carrier, allowing for high data transfer rates but limited flexibility in sharing bandwidth. On the other hand, MCPC enables multiple channels to be transmitted per carrier, providing greater bandwidth efficiency and flexibility for simultaneous data transfers. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences of users depending on their specific requirements.</p></p>
