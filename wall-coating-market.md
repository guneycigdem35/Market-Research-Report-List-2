<p><p>Wall coating market is divided into three main types: Solvent Type Coating, Emulsion Type Coating, and Inorganic Polymer Coating. Solvent Type Coating is made from organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process. Emulsion Type Coating uses water as the carrier for the paint, making it easier to clean up and eco-friendly. Inorganic Polymer Coating is known for its high durability and resistance to weathering and other external factors. Each type offers different benefits and characteristics to cater to various customer needs.</p></p>
