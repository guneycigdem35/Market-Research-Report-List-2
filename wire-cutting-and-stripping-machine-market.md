<p><p>Wire cutting and stripping machines are available in two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. These machines are suitable for small scale operations and offer more control over the stripping process. On the other hand, automatic machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention. They are ideal for high volume production and offer increased efficiency and speed in wire processing.</p></p>
