<p><strong>The Wireless Thermostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Battery-powered Wireless Thermostats</li><li>Hardwired Wireless Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless thermostats have two main market types: battery-powered and hardwired. Battery-powered wireless thermostats are powered by replaceable batteries, offering flexibility in installation without the need for electrical wiring. They are easy to install and can be placed anywhere in the home. On the other hand, hardwired wireless thermostats are connected directly to the home's electrical system, providing a reliable power source and constant connection to the HVAC system. Both types offer convenient remote access and control of heating and cooling systems.</p></p>
