<p><ul><li>Yeast Extracts</li><li>Yeast Autolysates</li><li>Yeast Beta-Glucan</li><li>Yeast Derivatives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The yeast ingredients market consists of various types such as yeast extracts, yeast autolysates, yeast beta-glucan, and yeast derivatives. Yeast extracts are produced by breaking down the cell walls of yeast cells to extract their contents. Yeast autolysates are created through the self-digestion of yeast cells. Yeast beta-glucan is a type of soluble fiber derived from yeast cell walls. Yeast derivatives are substances that are derived from yeast cells through various processes like extraction or fermentation. Each type of yeast ingredient offers unique nutritional benefits and functionality in food and beverage applications.</p></p>
