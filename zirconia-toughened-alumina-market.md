<p><strong>The Zirconia Toughened Alumina Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fibre Toughening</li><li>Particle Toughening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zirconia Toughened Alumina (ZTA) is a composite material used to improve the toughness and strength of ceramics. There are two main types of ZTA markets: fibre toughening and particle toughening. In fibre toughening, zirconia fibres are added to alumina to enhance its mechanical properties. In particle toughening, zirconia particles are dispersed within alumina to inhibit crack propagation and improve fracture resistance. Both types of ZTA provide enhanced toughness, wear resistance, and thermal stability compared to traditional ceramics.</p></p>
